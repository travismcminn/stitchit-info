What To Submit

Recommended Information
  • Machine brand and exact model
  • Number of heads and needles
  • Approximate year of purchase
  • Overall condition and any issues
  • Photos of front, side, control panel, and sewing area
  • List of included hoops, cap system, or accessories
  • Any known service history or major repairs
